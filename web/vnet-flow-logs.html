VNet Flow Logs Analyzer

⚙ Scanner configuration

Point the analyzer at a VNet flow log's storage account. Provide the storage account (and optionally the flow log resource id — used to derive the blob path prefix and resolve the storage account when it's left blank). Records are read straight out of blob storage for the chosen lookback window. This is a data-plane read: the signed-in identity needs Storage Blob Data Reader on the storage account, not just ARM Reader.
